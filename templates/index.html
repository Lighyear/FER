Facial Emotion Recognition

Welcome to the Facial Emotion Recognition (FER) Web App! This application uses a deep learning model to detect and classify emotions from facial expressions in real-time. The model is trained on a combined dataset of FER-2013 and CK+, capable of recognizing seven emotions: Angry, Disgust, Fear, Happy, Sad, Surprise, and Neutral.

To get started, click the button below to access the real-time emotion detection page. You can start the video feed, pause it, and close the session to save the recorded video and emotion data.
